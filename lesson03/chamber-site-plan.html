WDD230 Chamber Site Plan

Site Name: Chibanion

I named this site Chibanion based on my home place Chiba, and the term Union.

Site Purpose

This site is supposed to help for everyone in Chiba who's looking for local community, event, job, feature, and connection as a whole local community. This site would provide brief summary of events, jobs, upcoming community events, and some social infromations of the commerce.

Scenarios

  • Where can I search for the job?
  • How can I see the location?
  • Is there any events provided in the page?
  • Where can I see the contact information of commerce?
